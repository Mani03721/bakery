Use a Cake Saf.

A cake safe is a box designed to prevent cakes from sliding, melting, or falling during transportation. These durable boxes also protect cakes from elements like strong winds, rain, hail, intense heat, dust, or pollen, and they can be cleaned and reused multiple times.
